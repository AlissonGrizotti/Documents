## Extras for Project Pearl ##

- Maintainers are required to specify their build type (OFFICIAL/UNOFFICIAL) through their device trees [PEARL_BUILD_TYPE].

- Maintainers are expected to specify their name through their device trees [PEARL_MAINTAINER].

- Maintainers are expected to specify the CPU model name, with words seperated by a '_' [CPU_MODEL].

eg: 

CPU_MODEL := "Snapdragon_625"
PEARL_BUILD_TYPE := OFFICIAL
PEARL_MAINTAINER := Name_Surname / yourname
